# Task 2 – Logo Design

## Brand Name
NeuraTech

## Objective
Designed a minimalist technology logo representing innovation, intelligence, and futuristic digital solutions.

## Logo Variants
- Full Colour
- Monochrome (Black)

## Design Concept
The logo combines geometric shapes with the letter "N" to symbolize technology, connectivity, and innovation. Blue represents trust, intelligence, and modern digital solutions.
